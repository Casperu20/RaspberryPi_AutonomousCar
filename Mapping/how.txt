or too see the png live:

Run cropped view:

python3 visualize_grid_map.py

Run live cropped updates:

python3 visualize_grid_map.py --watch

Use a bigger crop margin:

python3 visualize_grid_map.py --margin 6

Show the full 61 x 61 map only when needed:

python3 visualize_grid_map.py --full

Then open the PNG:

xdg-open robot_grid_map.png

python3 visualize_grid_map.py --watch --margin 5
