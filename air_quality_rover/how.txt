How to run it

Start your robot mapper:

python3 autonomous_grid_mapper.py

In another terminal, watch the normal map:

python3 visualize_grid_map.py --watch --margin 5

In another terminal, watch the temperature heatmap:

python3 visualize_heatmap.py --field temperature --watch

And humidity:

python3 visualize_heatmap.py --field humidity --watch

Open the images:

xdg-open robot_grid_map.png
xdg-open temperature_heatmap.png
xdg-open humidity_heatmap.png
